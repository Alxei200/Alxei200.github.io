Spelunky Deathmatch Tourney Ruleset

This page has the default tourney ruleset and stagelist which has been reviewed by me and Kidfang.

The Ruleset

Lives: 99, Bombs and Rope: 1, Wins: 5

Stagelist

Mines

Miner Threat: BANNED (due to the whip almost certainly killing whoever you hit, and bombs killing off everyone if thrown correctly)

Layer Cake: COUNTERPICK (due to bomb + whip = death, but the opposing player still has a chance.

Rock n’ Roll: LEGAL (due to the rock being a pivotal and deadly part of the arena, but is still dodgeable)

Crates: LEGAL (due to no spikes or easy quick kill spots)

The Dome: BANNED (due to the only safe spot being the interior of the dome, and bombs lead to easy kills)

Falling Rocks: LEGAL (due to the rocks having little effect on the game)

The Spider Pit: COUNTERPICK (due to the spiders being pretty unfair, but the opposing player still has a chance)

Ladders: LEGAL (due to it being a simple neutral arena with no unfair easy deaths)

Jungle

Tiki Terror: COUNTERPICK (due to it being somewhat unfair with level generation, but it is sometimes fair.

Treehouses: BANNED (due to bombs killing off the trees extremely quick.

Vines: LEGAL (due to it being generally neutral)

Fortress: COUNTERPICK (due to it being easy to get stuck when bombs are placed outside of the fortress)

Dunk Tank: BANNED (due to it being too easy to get infinites)

Tarzan: BANNED (due to it being too similar to Miner Threat)

Kudzu: BANNED (due to the tiki towers and spikes)

The Hill: LEGAL (due to a pretty neutral start, and good for doubles and 4-player)

Ice

Ice Temple: COUNTERPICK (due to general chaos)

Moonbounce: BANNED (due to easy item infinites)

Don’t Fall!: BANNED (due to whips being OP)

The Calm: LEGAL (due to spikes being relatively difficult to get to without effort)

Deathbounce: BANNED (due to easy item infinites + spikes)

Frosted: COUNTERPICK (due to chaos, but you still have good control over space)

Cold Cage: LEGAL (due to it being a neutral and tactical stage)

Do Fall: BANNED (due to really easy kills once you get to the top)

Temple

Trap Temple: LEGAL (due to it being a tactical and hard stage)

Fire Pit: BANNED (due to whips being OP)

Rooms: COUNTERPICK (due to whips being OP, but running away is still an option)

Deathbox: COUNTERPICK (due to bombs being OP, but there is only really 4 bombs)

Pressure: BANNED (due to general chaos)

Crushers: BANNED (due to general chaos)

Pachinko: COUNTERPICK (due to whips being OP, but there is still options)

Fire Walk: ?? (unsure yet)
