GeoEngine

-COMING SOON-

GeoEngine is a Geometry Dash fan engine that will be open source for Game Maker 7/8 eventually. It currently features 1 fully functioning gamemode, 6 different orbs, 4 different pads, 2 different portals, 6 different speeds, and 1 level. More details in the text file below!

engine.txt

Levels

* Level 1: Intercept (uses Intercept 2 by dj-nate as music)

* Level 2: Paradise (uses Paradise on E by BOUNC3 as music)

* Level 3: Impact (uses Chaoz Impact by ParagonX9 as music)

* Level 4: Defcon (uses Defcon Zero by ParagonX9 as music)

* Level 5: Runaway (uses Runaway by DaGrahamCraka as music)
