Mission: Cataclysm

A list of videos and photos showing progress on completing Cataclysm

Update 1: First time past the first Wave

This was the beginning... At this point in time, I was kind of just fooling around with the level, so there was no actual pressure to beat the level. But man, my heart was pounding when I made it past the wave.

Update 2: Second time past the first Wave, and attempted the ship

At this point, I had seen some videos of what came after the wave, so I had an idea of what I was supposed to do if I ever made it to the ship. I still failed really hard at it though :P

Update 3: First Record on New Cataclysm

After Update 2, I didn't make a lot of significant progress. I made it past the ship once before, but died instantaneously afterwards, and didn't happen to record it. When 2.0 came out and the new version of cataclysm came out, beating it seemed even further out of my reach than before... But I still managed to do this somehow...

Update 4: Survived until the Ball in Old Cataclysm

Once the 2.0 Cataclysm was released, I was a little discouraged, so I tried to complete the older Cataclysm before attempting the newer one. I found that I had gotten a lot better as a player than when I had last played the level...

Update 5: My Biggest Leap in Skill yet

Just for shits and giggles, I tried to do 2.0 Cataclysm, and failed at the wave almost every time. But then, I fluked...
